Hold up - where does the number come from?

Using your location, we fetch weather data from weather underground and use the current date, the high and low temperature, the average wind speed, and the daily and instantaneous snowfall. We plug your data into a neural network trained with existing snow day data from schools in Syracuse, Rochester, and Buffalo.

Why is this better than other snow day calculators?

Because deep learning is a new thing and some pretty amazing problems can be solved using technologies such as neural nets. For example, Google uses neural networks to do the heavy lifting behind web translations.
